1. Introduction

At Gplex, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect and how we use it, while ensuring that your information remains anonymous and unidentifiable.

2. Data Collection

We do not directly collect user data through our application. All interactions are handled through OpenAI's API, which does not provide us with any personal information from the users. 3. Anonymity

Our application is designed to operate with complete anonymity. As we do not collect or handle personal data such as names, email addresses, or other identifiers, all interactions cannot be traced back to individual users.
